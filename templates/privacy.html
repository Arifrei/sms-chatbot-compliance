Overview

Ari Freilich Dev respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it for our SMS chatbot.

What We Collect

Phone Number Only. We collect your phone number when you text our service. We do not collect names, emails, or other personal data through the chatbot.

How We Use Your Number

We use your phone number solely to send replies related to your conversation with the chatbot.

No Unsolicited Messages (User-Initiated Only)

We never initiate contact. You opt in by texting us first. All subsequent messages are direct responses or follow-ups to your inquiry.

Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Opt-Out & Help

Reply STOP to stop receiving messages at any time. Reply HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply.

Retention

We retain your number only as long as necessary to operate the chatbot and comply with legal obligations. When no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
